Fulham

Fulham is a football club in the EFL Championship. It was established in the year 1879 by a church, and is the oldest club in London, above West Ham United. The owner right now is Shahid Khan, and

he has been the owner since the year 2013 in July, for the price of 150 million to 200 million. (Price is not realeased.)

League History

Fulham, right now, is in the EFL Championship, but has been in many other leagues. Last season, they came 18th in the Premier League, resulting in relegation from England's top flight to the second best

league, in England, the Championship. Fulham does have its ups and downs every now and then though, as they have had 5 spells in the 3rd Division, EFL League One, but they had them before the year 2000.

Since then, Fulham has been in the top two leagues, going up and down.
